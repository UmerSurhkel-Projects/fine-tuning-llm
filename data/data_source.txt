TECHGADGETS SUPPORT BOT - DATA SOURCE INFORMATION
==================================================

Dataset: Bitext Customer Support LLM Training Dataset
Source: Hugging Face
URL: https://huggingface.co/datasets/bitext/Bitext-customer-support-llm-chatbot-training-dataset

Dataset Statistics:
------------------
- Total Examples: 26,872
- Categories: 11
- Intents: 27
- Format: CSV with structured fields

Dataset Fields:
--------------
1. flags        - Language variation tags (B, Q, L, Z, P, E, M, W, I, N, C)
2. instruction  - Customer query/question
3. category     - High-level semantic category
4. intent       - Specific user intent
5. response     - Example assistant response

Categories:
-----------
1. ACCOUNT      - Account management (create, edit, delete, switch)
2. ORDER        - Order management (cancel, change, track, place)
3. SHIPPING     - Shipping and delivery options
4. PAYMENT      - Payment methods and processing
5. REFUND       - Refund requests and tracking
6. INVOICE      - Invoice management
7. FEEDBACK     - Customer complaints and feedback
8. CANCEL       - Cancellation requests
9. CONTACT      - Support contact information
10. DELIVERY    - Delivery status and tracking
11. SUBSCRIPTION - Newsletter and subscription management

Adaptation Process:
-------------------
The original dataset responses were adapted to TechGadgets by:
1. Replacing generic placeholders with TechGadgets-specific information
2. Injecting brand mentions (TechGadgets)
3. Adding company-specific policies:
   - 30-day money-back guarantee
   - Standard shipping: 3-5 business days
   - Express shipping: 2-day for $9.99
   - Support: 24/7 chat, Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM phone
   - Warranty: 1-year manufacturer warranty
   - Price matching available

Training/Validation Split:
--------------------------
- Training Examples: 400
- Validation Examples: 100
- Total: 500 examples

Selection Strategy:
-------------------
- Proportional category distribution
- Intent diversity
- Flag diversity (linguistic variations)
- Non-overlapping between train and validation sets

Output Format:
--------------
OpenAI Chat Fine-Tuning JSONL format with:
- system message: TechGadgets support assistant instructions
- user message: Customer query (instruction field)
- assistant message: Adapted response with TechGadgets branding
